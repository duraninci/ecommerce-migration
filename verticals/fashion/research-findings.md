## Key Spring/Summer 2026 Fashion Trends (From Vogue & Industry Sources)

### 1. **Fashion as Feeling**
- Personality-driven looks rooted in wearability
- Bold use of color and craft
- Statement skirts paired with simple tops
- Texture and dramatic proportion as focal points
- Brands: Chanel, Bottega Veneta, Loewe, Balenciaga, Khaite, Altuzarra

### 2. **Lady of Leisure**
- Elegant ease and soft, draped tailoring
- Robe-like silhouettes
- Long scarves as key styling detail
- Silk and satin fabrics
- Inspired by Amalfi coast elegance

### 3. **Literary Chic / Modern Prep**
- Functional staples: cardigans, pencil skirts, button-downs, V-neck sweaters
- Offbeat color combos and layered styling
- Element of surprise (contrasting colors, unexpected details)
- Vibrant leather gloves and accessories

### 4. **The Art of the In-Between**
- Clever, lightweight layering for transitional weather
- Cropped outerwear with shirting and silk scarves
- Creative weather-responsive dressing

### 5. **Mode Sportif**
- Nylon anoraks and technical bottoms
- Sporty staples styled with heels and tailoring
- Contrast between sporty and formal elements
- Citron and bright colors

### 6. **Scarf Styling**
- Blown-up prints and exaggerated proportions
- Multicolored and patterned silks
- Scarves as tops and dresses
- Wrapped around waist and knotted over shoulder

### 7. **Jazz Age**
- 1920s shift dress silhouette
- Drop-waist and relaxed, midi-length fit
- Modern fabrics and prints
- Elegant alternative to tailoring

### 8. **Utility Dressing**
- Uniform-inspired silhouettes
- Durable fabrics
- Functionality-rooted details
- Earth tones with unexpected contrasts
- Cargo pants, utility coats, belted pieces
