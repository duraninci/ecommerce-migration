# E-Commerce Migration Research Summary

## Key Findings from Research

### Optimum7 Key Insights
- Over 1,000 migrations completed
- 3,000,000+ pages migrated
- Migration requires years of programming expertise
- Common platforms: Shopify, BigCommerce, Magento, WooCommerce, VTEX, OpenCart, Volusion, 3DCart
- Critical focus: Custom functionality preservation, data mapping accuracy, SEO preservation
- Migration checklist items: product descriptions, customer order history, integrations, category descriptions, custom functionalities

### BigCommerce Migration Guide Key Points
- 46% of online retailers consider replatforming a top priority
- Migration strategies: Phased, SaaS, Headless
- Headless migrations achieve 40-60% faster load times
- 1-second page delay = 7% conversion drop
- B2B ecommerce migration spend projected at $4.2 billion in 2025
- Phased migrations reduce risk by allowing early testing
- Common migration triggers: performance issues, rising TCO, capability gaps

### Common Migration Challenges (from multiple sources)
- Poor data quality affects 84% of migrations
- Incorrect data mapping
- Data loss or corruption
- SEO impact and SERP ranking loss
- Compatibility challenges between platforms
- 404/401/403 error pages
- Custom functionality preservation
- Timeline and cost overruns

### Platform Categories
- **Open-source:** OpenCart, WooCommerce, Magento
- **Hosted SaaS:** Shopify, BigCommerce, Volusion, 3DCart
- **Plug-and-play:** Wix, Squarespace
- **Enterprise:** VTEX, Oracle Commerce, EpiServer, SAP Hybris

### Migration Timeline Data
- Shopify to BigCommerce: 10-14 weeks (typical), 6-8 weeks (clean migration)
- Phased migrations reduce risk
- Headless migrations offer faster innovation cycles

### Key Success Metrics
- Sell-through rates
- Page load times
- Conversion rate improvements
- Revenue impact
- Customer retention rates
- SEO ranking preservation
